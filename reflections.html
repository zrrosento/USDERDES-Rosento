Reflection 1: First Week of USERDES Class

Our class last week went great. We’ve talked about a glimpse about ourselves and we got to know each other even more with the help of Trello. The lesson we’ve tackled makes discover each other even more and we got to know how others think. Trello helps us to become more creative in a way in such free that we all got to choose on how can we design on our own profiles. In some ways that all of us would agree at the same design of the Trello even though we all have different sense of designs. My thoughts for our last weeks class is that it helped me to see the things that I don’t usually focus on such as the small details that I tend to just forget because I thought those aren’t really that necessary. The lesson makes me think in a more diverse perspectives, in such that whenever I have to design on something I should make it feasible and at least realistic rather than just an idea without proper basis. Just like thinking a rocket elevator for a thousand floors which is crazy. But overall, I appreciate how the others thinks and sees the designs that I do. It helps us also to improve each other’s work and we get to become even more innovative in terms of creating our own inventions on what are the things that we should consider when it comes to making things. Also, our lesson helps us see what are my classmates’ favorites or likes in an application. We even got to share it to everyone with consideration on what makes that application stand among the other ones that we thought are less good than we actually use on a daily basis. Such as looking at it’s User Interfaces or whether we could actually use the application in an offline or online basis. We’ve even got to share the games application that we like. Overall, I really like how our class went for the 1st week. It’s such a great start on such a broad topics that we could actually share our own ideas to everyone and give our opinion and criticize it.

Overall, I really like how our class went for the 1st week. It’s such a great start on such a broad topics that we could actually share our own ideas to everyone and give our opinion and criticize it.
